Art Videos

Nathan T.

Video editing has been in my life for a long time, it's much cooler when you can implement an idea not only in one frame, but also in 24, and even hide advertising in the 25th frame) However, there are not many works to show yet, but this can be fixed, and a lot of work is already being done on this. So follow me, it will be even more interesting.

Photoshop. My experience in Photoshop is more than 3 years, I try to devote quite a lot of free time to this tool. All the works above were implemented with its help. During this time, I have honed many methods, ways and tools to mastery. But there is no limit to perfection and I will continue) limit overflow.

Premiere Pro. I use it most often to glue ready-made video fragments into a single composition. However, this is not the only thing I love about this program. Overlaying video effects, adding your own music to create a full-fledged work is also done in this application, as well as color correction and adding filters. I love it, period.

After effects. I try to be friends with this application, although it is difficult at first. Its interface is as clear as assembler (a programmer's joke), but it allows you to create something incredible. Create video effects, semi-3D parts, cool animations and even special effects for video clips. Together, this application is like a good wine. It is difficult to understand at first, but then the bouquet opens up and it is impossible to resist. Yes, I can be poetic.
